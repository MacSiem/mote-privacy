Privacy Policy — Mote

Last updated: April 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Mote is a mobile app for couples that helps share everyday moments of appreciation for your partner. Privacy is the foundation of the product: data is end-to-end encrypted, the app is offline-first, and most content never leaves your device in a form readable by anyone other than you and your partner.

App operator: Maciej Sieminski (contact: maciek.sieminski@gmail.com).

2. Data we process

2.1 Entry content and notes

Entries, messages, tags, mood check-ins and photos/audio you add are stored locally on your device and encrypted both at rest (local encryption) and in transit to your partner (end-to-end encryption). The app operator has no access to your entry content.

2.2 Pairing identifiers

Mote pairs two devices using a 6-character code or QR code. No email, phone number, or PII is required to use the app. Pairing produces a random identifier that itself does not identify a person.

2.3 Diagnostic data (Sentry)

To detect crashes and improve stability we use Sentry (sentry.io). Sentry collects:

  • error stack traces (crash logs);
  • device information (model, OS version, locale);
  • app version and build number.

Sentry does not collect entry content, usernames, or pair data. Diagnostic data retention: up to 90 days.

2.4 Advertising (AdMob)

Mote shows non-invasive banner ads in Settings, Empty state and Export screens. Ads are served by Google AdMob, whose SDK:

  • collects the Advertising ID (Android Advertising ID / Apple IDFA), shared with Google to serve ads;
  • collects diagnostic data (device model, country-level location, language).

We never sell personal data to third parties. You can reset your Advertising ID in Android/iOS settings at any time. Disable ads permanently via the one-time "Remove Ads" IAP.

2.5 Device permissions

  • Camera (CAMERA): take photos for entries (optional).
  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO): record voice messages (optional).
  • Photos/Media (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES): add images from gallery (optional).
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): gentle mood check-in reminders.
  • Vibrate (VIBRATE): haptic UI feedback.

Each permission is requested contextually and can be denied without losing access to core features.

3. Legal basis (GDPR)

For EU users: performance of contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) — core app features; legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) — Sentry diagnostics; consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — personalized ads (you may decline on first launch; non-personalized ads are default).

4. Your rights

You have the right to: access your data, rectify it, erase it, restrict processing, object to processing, and data portability. The app offers a full export (JSON + Markdown) at any time, free of charge. Account deletion removes all pair data from the backend; local data remains until you uninstall the app.

To exercise data subject rights or file a complaint, email maciek.sieminski@gmail.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your data protection authority.

5. Children

Mote is not intended for children under 13. Google Play target audience is 13+. We do not knowingly collect data from children; if identified, it will be deleted promptly.

6. Changes

Material changes to this policy will be announced in-app and on this page (with the updated date at the top). Continued use of Mote after changes means acceptance.
